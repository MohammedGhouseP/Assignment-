Welcome to my personal portfolio website. I have designed this space to showcase my skills, projects, and experience as a web developer. Whether you're a recruiter, client, or fellow developer, I hope you find something here that sparks your interest. I believe in continuous learning, creativity, and building solutions that make an impact. This site is built with simplicity in mind, and every section is crafted to reflect my journey in tech.

Over the past few years, I have worked on various projects ranging from static websites to full-stack web applications. I started my journey with HTML and CSS, exploring the fundamentals of web design. Slowly, I transitioned into JavaScript, which opened the door to making websites dynamic and interactive. These experiences laid a strong foundation for my current work in React.js, Node.js, and other modern technologies.

One of my favorite projects is a responsive e-commerce platform where users can browse products, add them to a cart, and complete purchases. I implemented user authentication, integrated a payment gateway, and managed product data using MongoDB. This project helped me understand the complexities of building scalable applications and the importance of clean code and modular design.

In addition to development, I also have a keen eye for UI/UX. I use tools like Figma to prototype and design interfaces before bringing them to life in code. I focus on accessibility and performance to ensure that every website I build is inclusive and runs smoothly across all devices and browsers. These practices reflect my commitment to delivering a great user experience.

Collaboration and teamwork have been central to my growth. I've worked in both academic and professional environments where I've contributed to team projects using Git and GitHub. These experiences have taught me how to work effectively in Agile settings, manage tasks with tools like Trello and Jira, and communicate technical ideas clearly with teammates.
